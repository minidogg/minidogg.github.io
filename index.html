What I believe

I believe the web should be simpler and rely less on JavaScript on the front-end for everything when most cases they can just render on the server side.

Speaking from experience slow and bloated sites can bog down low end machine including school computers that should have no reason to need state of the art CPUs, GPUs, etc

I would happily like to announce this site doesn't require JavaScript to function and doesn't use a bloated framework. Go ahead and try it. Install an extension to disable JavaScript, like uBlock Origin, or just disable it in your browser settings.
